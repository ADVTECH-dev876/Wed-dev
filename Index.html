Gentle Care for Every Stage

From adolescence through menopause—and especially on the path to parenthood—we support your whole health with evidence-based, minimally invasive options.

Fertility Care

Personalized evaluation and treatment including ovulation induction, IUI, IVF/ICSI, and management of complex cases like azoospermia with TESA.

PCOD & Hormonal Health

Compassionate management of PCOD, irregular periods, and hormonal imbalances with lifestyle integration and medical support tailored to your goals.

Preventive Gynecology

Well-woman check-ups, Pap smears, breast exams, and management of menstrual disorders in a judgment-free, comfortable environment.

Fertility Preservation

Egg freezing and fertility counseling for women planning ahead due to medical treatments, career timelines, or personal readiness.

Minimally Invasive Surgery

Laparoscopy and hysteroscopy for diagnosis and treatment with faster recovery, less discomfort, and minimal scarring.

Preconception Counseling

Optimize your health before pregnancy with personalized guidance on nutrition, supplements, lifestyle, and managing existing conditions.

Dr. Ambily has specialized training in male infertility management, including complex cases like azoospermia (no sperm in ejaculate). We offer comprehensive semen analysis, hormonal evaluation, and procedures like TESA (testicular sperm extraction) when needed—all coordinated with your partner's comfort and dignity in mind.
