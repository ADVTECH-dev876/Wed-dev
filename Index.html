Meet Dr. Ambily Jose

AIIMS-trained specialist dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based care for women's health and fertility challenges

AJ

Dr. Ambily Jose

MBBS, MD OBG (Gold Medalist), DNB, FNB | AIIMS New Delhi

Dr. Ambily Jose is a distinguished Gynecologist and Fertility Specialist with extensive training from AIIMS New Delhi. Her journey in women's healthcare began with a gold medal in her MD at one of India's most prestigious medical institutions.

With advanced expertise in Assisted Reproductive Technology (IVF, ICSI) and male infertility management, Dr. Jose brings a perfect blend of scientific rigor and compassionate care to every patient interaction. She believes that understanding each woman's unique story is the foundation of effective treatment.

Professional Qualifications

  • MBBS from AIIMS New Delhi
  • MD in Obstetrics & Gynecology (Gold Medalist) from AIIMS New Delhi
  • DNB in Obstetrics & Gynecology
  • FNB in Reproductive Medicine
  • Advanced training in Laparoscopy and Hysteroscopy
  • Specialized expertise in male infertility including TESA for azoospermia

Dr. Jose's approach combines cutting-edge medical knowledge with genuine empathy, ensuring that every patient feels heard, respected, and supported throughout their healthcare journey.
